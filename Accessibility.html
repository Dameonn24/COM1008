Accessibility Statement

As I was designing my website, I tried to make it as accessible as possible. For example, adding alt text to images, making sure that there is a large contrast between my content and the background and menu options are keyboard-focusable. If there is anything that could help me make the website more acccessible. Please use the contact form and I'll try my best to make the changes.
